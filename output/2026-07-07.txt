## 1. Top AI News

1. **Savi's app to protect consumers from AI scams**: Savi has raised $7 million in seed funding to launch an app that protects consumers from realistic AI scams, such as kidnappers demanding ransom. **Why it matters:** This app's launch highlights the growing need for AI-driven security solutions to combat increasingly sophisticated scams.
2. **American autonomous ground vehicles in Ukraine**: Forterra has deployed over 100 self-driving ATVs in conflict zones in Ukraine, marking a significant milestone in autonomous vehicle deployment. **Why it matters:** This deployment demonstrates the potential of autonomous vehicles in high-stakes environments, with implications for future military and civilian applications.
3. **AI-run ransomware attack still needed human intervention**: The first known AI-run ransomware attack required human setup and infrastructure, revealing that AI-driven cybercrime is not yet fully autonomous. **Why it matters:** This incident highlights the ongoing need for human oversight and intervention in AI-driven systems, particularly in high-risk areas like cybersecurity.
4. **SK Hynix's AI-driven boom**: SK Hynix is experiencing a significant boom due to AI, with plans for a multibillion-dollar US IPO. **Why it matters:** This development underscores the growing importance of AI in driving business growth and investment opportunities.
5. **Vercel CEO on splitting models from agents**: Guillermo Rauch discusses the importance of optimizing AI models for production and separating them from agents. **Why it matters:** This insight highlights the need for AI architecture optimization to ensure efficient and effective deployment of AI models.
